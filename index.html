We are Spectrum VR

Virtual Reality provides the ability to create immersive gaming situations. The current SpectrumVR project is a game that incorporates a component designed to help children with autism build social skills. Our goal at SpectrumVR is to create a fun social gaming experience with lasting value. Our VR technology is provided by Oculus, our corporate parner.
