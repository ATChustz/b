We are Spectrum VR

Virtual Reality provides immersive situations in which children with autism can build social skills in safe environments. Our goal at SpectrumVR is to extend current child autism therapies in new ways using virtual reality and technology developed by Oculus, our corporate parner.

Engage With Us

Immerse

SpectrumVR virtual reality simulations provide realistic experiences in which children remember what they practice.

Embolden

The simulations are safe spaces in which children can practice social skills without the high stakes of the real world.

Empower

The interactivity of SpectrumVR’s simulations allow for improvisation in a way that bolsters confidence and social prowess.
